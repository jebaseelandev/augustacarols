About this Event

Augusta carols brings to you the greatest opportunity to explore your singing talent. Solo singing by All.

Rules of this Event

  • Every Participants will be judged individually.
  • Songs should be in English or Tamil.
  • Instrument can be used (optional)
  • Whatsapp us the video shot on your mobile phone.(any kind of editing will lead to disqualification)
  • Song Videos can be of maximum 4 mins
  • Use of karaoke is not allowed.
  • Last date of submission is 15th Nov,2020
  • Donot upload your song videos to any public platforms like Youtube/facebook etc before 25th Dec 2020.
